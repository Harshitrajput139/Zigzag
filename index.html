Travel Terms

  • Trip:
    This is the most common and general term for travel. A trip involves going from one place to another and then returning, and can be for various reasons, from a short excursion to a longer vacation.
  • Voyage:
    A voyage specifically refers to travel by sea or, in modern terms, also by space. The word is derived from Latin, relating to provisions for a long journey, and the term implies a significant, often long, journey.
  • Tour:
    A tour is a journey made for enjoyment or to visit specific sights. Tours can be organized for a specific number of people or for a particular purpose, like visiting different attractions.
